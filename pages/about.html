Sakkasenta

Sakkasenta (サッカーセンター / Sakkāsentā / Soccer Center) is an application that provides soccer sports information. You can see the current competition, such as the Premier League, Bundesliga, Ligue 1 and many more. You can also see detailed information about a team, such as team match schedule, team squad. And not only that, you can also add your favorite team in this app !!
